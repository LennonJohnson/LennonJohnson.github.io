Analysis of the Impact of the Inflation Reduction Act on Inflation | Python, Pandas, Plotly

Analyze the impact the Inlaftion Reduction Act (IRA) had on inflation using a python notebook. Using inflation data from the Fred API I forecasted inflation using the trends from before the IRA. I then compared the forecast with the actual data to understand the impact of the legislation. The results were an average of 6.7 reduction points from the forecast and a 5.81 percentage point reduction from the lower bound of the estimates. This data informs policy makers on the effectiveness of the IRA policies to guide future decisions.
